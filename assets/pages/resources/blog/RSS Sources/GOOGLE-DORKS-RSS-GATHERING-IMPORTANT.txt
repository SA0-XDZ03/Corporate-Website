GOOGLE DORKING - RSS FEED GATHERING QUERIES
===========================================
BASIC TARGETED RSS/XML FEEDS: 
> inurl:"/feed" OR inurl:"/rss" OR inurl:"/atom" OR inurl:"rss.xml" OR inurl:"feed.xml" OR inurl:"atom.xml"
WITH TARGETED KEYWORDS: 
> (inurl:"/feed" OR inurl:"/rss" OR inurl:"rss.xml" OR inurl:"feed.xml") (intitle:"military" OR intitle:"defense" OR intitle:"intel" OR intitle:"intelligence" OR intitle:"osint" OR intitle:"cybersecurity" OR intitle:"cyber warfare")
XML FILES ONLY (Tight focus): 
> filetype:xml (inurl:"rss" OR inurl:"feed") (military OR defense OR intel OR osint OR cyber)
KNOWN CMS-BASED RSS FEED PATTERNS: 
> inurl:"/category/intelligence/feed" OR inurl:"/category/military/feed" OR inurl:"/category/cybersecurity/feed"
ADVANCED REFINED WITH SITE TYPES: 
> (Think tanks, blogs, orgs)inurl:feed OR inurl:rss (site:.mil OR site:.org OR site:.int OR site:.gov OR site:medium.com OR site:wordpress.com) (military OR osint OR cyber)
AGGREGATORS / OSINT HUBS / NEWS SOURCES:
> inurl:"/rss" (intitle:"OSINT" OR intitle:"Cybersecurity" OR intitle:"Defense Intelligence")
